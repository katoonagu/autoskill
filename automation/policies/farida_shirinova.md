# Farida Shirinova Policy

Source of truth for commercial outreach policy under `Autoskill`.

Files:
- Machine-readable policy: `automation/policies/farida_shirinova.yaml`
- Loader: `automation/policies/__init__.py`

What this policy fixes:
- formalizes brand categories and exclusions
- formalizes `instagram-native` exception handling
- formalizes channel tone and CTA rules
- marks brands with `1M+` followers as `ultra_premium`

Core principles:
- We sell advertising reach, awareness, women audience, recommendations, and image.
- We do not promise sales, clicks, or ROI.
- Instagram-native brands are acceptable if the visual/account level is high enough.
- `ultra_premium` brands should be recorded separately for special handling and other products.

Primary metrics to sell:
- `27.5M` views over 30 days
- `91.9%` of views from recommendations
- Moscow-heavy women audience

Preferred CTA:
- offer current stats
- offer `2–3` integration ideas
- optionally ask for PR/partnership contact
