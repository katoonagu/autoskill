# Control Plane

Supervisor-level orchestration across discovery, intelligence, review, planning, approvals, and conversation.

## What success looks like

- Tasks move out of inbox and into the right agent lane.
- Review and approval queues do not grow stale.
- Downstream stages receive the artifacts they expect.
